Jewelry Care Guide

Preserve the beauty of your Lovelie ear clips with our comprehensive care instructions.

Daily Care

  • Handle your ear clips with clean, dry hands to prevent oil and dirt transfer.
  • Remove ear clips before showering, swimming, or applying beauty products.
  • Store individually in the provided pouch to prevent scratches.

Cleaning

  • Gently wipe with a soft, lint-free cloth after each wear.
  • For deeper cleaning, use a mild soap solution and soft brush.
  • Dry thoroughly with a soft cloth before storing.

Storage

  • Keep in the original Lovelie pouch or a soft-lined jewelry box.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid storing with other jewelry to prevent tangling and scratches.

What to Avoid

  • Exposure to perfumes, lotions, and cosmetics.
  • Contact with water, especially chlorinated or salt water.
  • Harsh chemicals, including cleaning products.

Professional Care

For the best care of your Lovelie ear clips, we recommend professional cleaning every 6-12 months. Contact our customer service team to schedule a professional cleaning or repair service.
